Main Companions

Miroku (弥み勒ろく) is a hōshi who traveled with Inuyasha, Kagome, Shippō, and Sango.
Sango (珊瑚さんご) was the only teenage female yōkai taijiya from her village.
Shippō (七しっ宝ぽう) was an orphan kitsune, his father was killed by another yōkai.
Kirara(雲母きらら) is Sango's faithful nekomata companion and main method of transportation.
